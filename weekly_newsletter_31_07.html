Top 5 AI News of the Week

1

How to Disable X's Data Sharing for AI Training: X (ex-Twitter) enabled a default setting that allows companies to train their Grok AI on users’ posts. Still, you can turn it off and delete your conversation history through the desktop version. To disable this, click Settings > Privacy and Safety> Grok, uncheck the box and keep your data safe.

2

SearchGPT - AI-Powered Search Engine Prototype: OpenAI's new AI-powered search engine, SearchGPT, provides organized, real-time information and is initially available to 10,000 test users. The goal is to integrate SearchGPT into ChatGPT, posing a challenge to Google and competitors like Perplexity.

3

Runway’s AI Video Generator Trained on YouTube and Pirated Content: Runway's AI video generator, Gen-3 Alpha, was trained using thousands of YouTube videos and pirated films from major companies like Netflix and Disney, according to 404 Media. A former employee revealed that a web crawler used proxies to download videos, raising legal and ethical concerns.

4

ZoomInfo Alum Raises $15M for AI Sales Engineer Startup: Arjun Pillai, former ZoomInfo chief data officer, launched DocketAI in August 2023 to assist non-technical salespeople with technical queries. DocketAI recently raised $15 million in Series A funding and integrates data from over 100 applications to enhance sales productivity.

5

Meta Introduces 'Imagine Me' AI Selfie Generator: Meta's new feature allows users to generate AI selfies as avatars like astronauts or superheroes using the Llama 3.1 model. Unlike previous viral AI avatar apps, Meta offers this feature for free, likely leading to a surge in AI-generated selfies.

CX Genie Weekly News & Updates

We are excited to share some fantastic updates and new features we've rolled out to enhance CX Genie for you. Here's what's new:

🔗 New Integrations

Instagram

After a long review process, our much-anticipated Instagram integration is finally live! This integration allows you to manage and respond to Instagram messages directly from CX Genie, enhancing your social media customer support capabilities.

Follow these steps to integrate your businesses Instagram with CX Genie within minutes:

Workspace → Bots → Development → Platform Integration → Instagram (follow the 2 steps to add your page and select your bot)

🌟 New Features

Activity Log

  • Helps you keep track of all system activities, ensuring transparency and accountability in your team’s actions. 
  • How to Benefit: Access the Activity Log in workspace settings to monitor changes in ticket status, agent assignments, bot management, and more.

⬆️ Updates

Help Desk

  • All existing conversations and tickets are now on the Help Desk screen.
  • Customizable Sidebar: The sidebar classifies conversations/tickets, and users can customize the view according to their preferences.
  • Benefit: Enhances the efficiency of large support teams by providing a comprehensive and customizable view of all activities.
  • How to Benefit: Utilize the new Help Desk view to quickly navigate and manage conversations and tickets, adapting the sidebar to your workflow preferences.

Contacts (Previously Chat Users)

  • Chat Users have now ben renamed to Contacts.
  • New Logic: Contacts are now divided into two types:
    • Users: Those who have provided an email and phone
    • Leads: Those who have not provided an email or phone
  • Benefit: This update helps better categorize and manage your contacts, making it easier to follow up with leads and users. 

Team Management Update

  • You can now assign team leads to manage your teams better. 
  • Benefit: Streamlines team management by assigning leadership roles and improving team coordination and accountability. 

Categories & Tags

  • Tag Section Move: The tag section has moved from the user chat to the category section and will be used for chats and tickets.
  • How to Benefit: Use tags consistently across chats and tickets to improve your ability to locate and categorize interactions across your team.
