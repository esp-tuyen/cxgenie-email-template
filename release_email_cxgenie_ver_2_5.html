CX Genie Version 2.5 Release: Exciting Enhancements!

Dear CX Genie Users,

 

We are delighted to announce the latest release of CX Genie – Version 2.5! This update brings significant enhancements to improve your experience on our platform. So what are they?

✨ Document Storage Limit Bar:

  • A new storage limit bar is now displayed for each account, ensuring efficient resource management.
  • The maximum storage for each chatbot has been set at 5GB, providing ample space for your training data.


✨ Increased Document Upload Limit:

  • We have increased the maximum document file upload limit to 100MB.
  • This enhancement allows you to incorporate a broader range of training data, leading to more comprehensive chatbots.


✨ Enhanced Document Upload Handling:

  • A queue-based approach to file upload processing, ensuring orderly and thorough training for all your documents.


✨ Document Learning Status Display:

  • The status of document learning is now visible, offering enhanced transparency.
  • Users can easily track the processing progress for their bots' updated information.

 

Additionally, you can access the Release section to stay updated on our significant enhancement!
